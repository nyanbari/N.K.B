Extraction Of Oil And Biodiesel From Senna Tora Seed And Its Optimization

The outcome of an extraction of oil and biodiesel from Senna tora seed and optimization project is to gain knowledge and skills in the extraction of oil from Senna tora seeds, its suitability for biodiesel production, and the optimization of the extraction process. The objective is to provide a comprehensive understanding of the extraction techniques, optimization factors, and biodiesel production process, with the goal of improving the efficiency and sustainability of the oil extraction process.

Thermal Design of Evaporative Condenser

The outcome of a thermal design of evaporative condenser project is to gain knowledge and skills in the design and optimization of evaporative condensers for industrial cooling systems. The objective is to provide students with an understanding of heat transfer principles, simulation techniques such as computational fluid dynamics (CFD), and the optimization of system performance. The outcome includes the ability to perform thermal design, analysis, and system optimization to ensure efficient and effective operation of evaporative condensers.
